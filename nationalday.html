National Cinnamon Crescent Day

Today is all about Cinnamon Crescents!

April 10, 2026 Sam Tun

Today is April 10th, and it's National Cinnamon Crescent Day! These sweet treats sound amazing!

Sorry that I missed yesterday's post, I was trying to get the website to run and other things, but I got put really behind schedule. New updates to the website will be made and worked on hard and released on April 12th, so there may be no new posts for a day. But on the topic of cinnamon crescents, they are deliciously baked desserts filled with not more than just plain sugar and the cinnamon for it's signature cinnamon taste. Once baked until you have that golden brown edge and nice crispy texture, you can add icing for some more sweetness! The best time to enjoy them would be right as they come out of the oven, since the longer you have them out, they will probably become stale much quicker. I could imagine having one while sitting cozy by a campfire and enjoying it with a warm glass of milk.
